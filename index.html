About Us

Welcome to Berkeley Barber Shop, an icon of Berkeley Heights, New Jersey since 1986. With a rich legacy deeply rooted in our community, we take pride in redefining and elevating personal style. Our skilled barbers, with decades of experience, provide precision cuts that stand the test of time. At Berkeley Barber Shop, we go beyond grooming – we craft personalized experiences that amplify your confidence. Step into a tradition of excellence, where classic meets contemporary, and let us redefine your style journey.
